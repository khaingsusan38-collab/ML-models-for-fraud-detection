Liu (2024) compared logistic regression, random forest and XGBoost on over one million banking transactions, finding that XGBoost consistently outperformed the other models across key metrics including ROC-AUC and F1-score. Similarly, Patil et al. (2024) evaluated multiple data balancing techniques alongside the same model set, reporting that the combination of random oversampling with XGBoost achieved the highest F1-score. These findings provide a basis for the model selection in this study.
